When going on a trip there are certain preparations we have to make if we want to be successful in getting there. We need a sense of where we’re going, how we’re going to get there, and the challenges we might face along the way.

When it's not about you

It seems like we’re always striving, even fighting, against others for some degree of accomplishment, popularity, or accumulation to feel like we’ve “made it” in this world. We get one thing/relationship/award, and then it’s on to the next one. We can become so self-focused that we forget to see the whole picture. James 4 encourages us to have a accurate perspective of the world and ourselves. Instead of making ourselves the center of the universe, he reminds us that it's much bigger than us, revolving around someone else.
